Our Services

DRONE PRODUCTION FOR SPECIAL PURPOSE PROJECTS

We provide R&D, design, production and field support for special project drones equipped with a variety of hardware, software and flight features in areas that our customers need for use in activities such as intelligence, observation, search and rescue, agriculture, fire, mapping.

 (*You can review some of our products we have developed the sale section) 


CONSULTING

We provide joint work, equipment, consultancy, maintenance and services within the scope of cooperation with universities, private or public institutions operating in similar fields.


EDUCATION 

We provide certified drone operator training to our existing customers, and people or institutions in need. 

Lithium Ion (Li-Ion) BATTERY PRODUCTION

For project drones, model aircraft or devices that need Lithium Ion energy efficiency, we manufacture Li-Ion batteries from 2S~8S with long life, high safety and high discharge capacity and up to 30C discharge. Our products use original Japanese cells. All batteries are produced by testing to give the current and capacity values on them in full.
